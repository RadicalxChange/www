Hi RadxChanger,

Lots of people feel that the times are getting away from us. That what is happening to the economy, to politics, to culture, is confusing, accelerating, and out of our control. AI is one of the big reasons why. The technology will affect peoples' lives and our social fabric for the foreseeable future, yet besides the technologists building it, few people understand their role in the system or how they can meaningfully participate in shaping its future.  
  
This year RadicalxChange is focused on changing this. We’re building concrete pilots that demonstrate the importance of human contributions to AI and that shape its economic and cultural impacts.  
  
To this end, we have launched an exciting collaboration with [OpenMined](https://openmined.org/), a leading developer of machine learning and privacy-focused data sharing tools, to preserve a marketplace for high-quality information in the AI economy. Starting with the publishing industry, we're empowering them to manage AI training on their content _collectively_, instead of individual providers offering their data on a fragmented basis.  
  
We call this "federated data bargaining" and we've begun prototyping it with several media partners. As you can see in the screenshots below, a simple Wordpress plugin that OpenMined has built allows media producers to easily join the network and delegate management of third-party access through a RAG to a [trusted intermediary](https://www.radicalxchange.org/media/blog/three-pathways-to-distributed-power-in-the-ai-economy/), such as a trade association or even RadicalxChange.

![](/images/announcements/newsletters/2025-04-federated-data-bargaining-with-openmined/3bac6d5e-3b9d-85e0-3c7c-6aedb6e29632.png)

![](/images/announcements/newsletters/2025-04-federated-data-bargaining-with-openmined/c8ff6e7e-a7f9-cc3d-a2a2-2a5b032c944a.png)

![](/images/announcements/newsletters/2025-04-federated-data-bargaining-with-openmined/39c743e2-183f-2197-44c9-a6dd6167deb5.png)

We have big hopes for this pilot. We think it can become the definitive framework for AI access to protected content, setting a template for many industries to follow in order to retain lasting economic traction in the age of AI.   
  
But we have to move fast. We're competing with [venture-backed startups](https://www.prorata.ai/) that want to put all of this behind a walled garden, and eventually get acquired by Big Tech. Success will require putting together major sectoral-scale partnerships that keep power in the hands of content providers, consumers, and other originators of important formation.  
  
So you can expect to hear lots more about this over the coming months. In the meantime, please do reach out if you are connected with any news media organizations, or other data-holders, or any lawyers, regulators or funders, who might be interested in this pilot program. Thanks!

## The first beta cycle of Fairclouds is now live!

[Fairclouds](http://fairclouds.life) is a project designed to bring financial and ethical support to the Indigenous Communities of Salinas Grandes and Laguna de Guayatayoc in the North of Argentina in the fight against lithium extraction on their ancestral lands. Fairclouds brings to life the economic model we call partial common ownership in support of this cause.
