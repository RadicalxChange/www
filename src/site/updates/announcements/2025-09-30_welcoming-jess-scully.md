Hello RadxChanger,

We are beyond thrilled to announce an expansion of our leadership team: **Jess Scully has officially joined RxC as our new Executive Director!** With a background in activism and advocacy, creative industries, local government and city-making, Jess is the kind of plural thinker who is right at home in our movement. Her book, [_Glimpses of Utopia: Real Ideas for a Fairer World_](https://glimpsesofutopia.com/), documents the kinds of social movements and transformative tools we champion through the RxC network. Welcome, Jess! 

![](/images/announcements/newsletters/2025-09-welcoming-jess-scully/5c577b2e-b12b-e964-2892-7f842cd34cef.jpeg)

You can learn more about Jess on her website, [jessscully.com](https://www.jessscully.com/).

With Jess onboard, our team has been developing a new set of **Strategic Pillars** that will focus our efforts in 2026 and beyond. We see these Pillars as the foundation of systemic transformation, which will guide us as we plan new activities across political, technological, cultural and social domains. We're excited to share these with you tomorrow on our Community Call, and to hear your feedback. We hope you can join us.   
  
As part of this process of renewal, **we also invite all of you to reflect and help us co-create a new articulation of the core values guiding our work**. We are launching a collaborative process on [Agora Citizen Network](https://agoracitizen.network/feed/conversation/4OcpxQ), where you can add your ideas and feedback throughout October. Click the link below and create an account to get started!

[What values draw you to RadicalxChange?](https://agoracitizen.network/feed/conversation/4OcpxQ "What values draw you to RadicalxChange?")

We will present the outcomes of this process on our November Community Call, and ask for your contributions to our next program of projects and activity for 2026. We hope you can participate!
