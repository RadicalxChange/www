OK, now on to the news.  
  
Glen's [debate with Curtis Yarvin](https://opentodebate.org/debate/should-the-u-s-be-ruled-by-a-ceo-dictator/) is finally upon us. They take the stage in New York City this Thursday night. Take a moment now to make sure you've bought your [ticket](https://support.opentodebate.org/event/should-the-us-be-ruled-by-a-ceo-dictator/e710643) or registered for the [livestream](https://www.workcast.com/register?cpak=1727884069847863), because you will NOT want to miss this.  
  
**Everyone can chat about the debate by joining the online discussion on [Context Engine](https://contextengine.xyz/demo/rxc)**, a new tool developed by RxC community member Charlie Thompson. Try it out and let us know what you think!  
  
We're also helping organize watch parties for the debate, like we did for the release of [Good Enough Ancestor](https://www.radicalxchange.org/media/blog/soft-determination-wins/) six months ago. Some cities like [Denver](https://luma.com/48qhea9m), [Montreal](https://www.instagram.com/p/DN_guhkD5SC/?igsh=MWJkd3llZm5lcWNsaA%3D%3D), [La Paz](https://luma.com/ln7no8gv), and [Sydney](https://lu.ma/mbedd2o7?locale=en-AU) will stream it live, while others like [Paris](https://lu.ma/usz8kaq2), [Zurich](https://luma.com/eood5fxm), and [Barcelona](https://lu.ma/tmc79a1g) (where it will be the middle of the night) are meeting up this weekend to watch. **It's not too late to set up a watch party!** Reach out and we can help you.

## From Around the Pluriverse

→ [Plurality & The 6-Pack of Care](https://sharing.ia.net/presenter/15d789cbe26c453ba047726600699b81/view): A presentation by Audrey Tang for DeepMind. "The vertical narrative of a technological 'Singularity' needs a horizontal alternative. Today, I wish to discuss that alternative: a steering wheel called ⿻ Plurality, and its design principles: the 6-Pack of Care."  
  
→ [The Renovator](https://therenovator.substack.com/): A new Substack by Danielle Allen and collaborators. "The Renovator is a voice for those ready to build, not tear down... Whether you're a policymaker or a high school teacher, a democracy nerd or a curious newcomer, a conservative reformer or a progressive pragmatist, there's a seat for you here."  
  
→ [The D/acc Pathway: A World Shaped by Decentralized, Democratic, Defensive Acceleration](https://ai-pathways.existentialhope.com/the-d-acc-2035-scenario/): A speculative vision for the coming decade of AI by the Existential Hope program at Foresight Institute. "We can and should be selective about which technologies we accelerate, prioritizing those that strengthen defense, distribute power, and promote cooperation."  
  
→ [How Taiwan is Using Technology to Defend Itself](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l6LRrzAjDC8): A podcast by Reid Hoffman with Audrey Tang and Divya Siddarth. "Taiwan is one of the most geopolitically essential players in AI. But now, Taiwan must plan for a world where cyberattacks and deepfakes are as dangerous as missiles. Fmr Taiwan Digital Minister Audrey Tang breaks down how Taiwan protects itself in the 21st century."  
  
→ [Google And Pollster Scott Rasmussen Will Use AI To Survey Americans’ Political Views](https://www.forbes.com/sites/richardnieva/2025/08/14/inside-googles-plan-to-use-ai-to-survey-americans-on-their-political-views/?utm_source=ForbesMainTwitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=socialflowForbesMainTwitter): An article by Richard Nieva in Forbes. "A collaboration between the tech giant and a polling nonprofit will gather five to 10 people each from every congressional district to answer questions about the nation’s most pressing issues. If it works, it could upend the fraught world of political polling."  
  
→ [Humanocracy, Updated and Expanded: Creating Organizations as Amazing as the People Inside Them](https://www.amazon.com/Humanocracy-Revised-Updated-Creating-Organizations/dp/1647826373): A new edition of the _Wall Street Journal_ bestseller by Gary Hamel and Michele Zanini that draws on and highlights Audrey's work in Taiwan as a model for the world.
